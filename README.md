# Truck Rental AI Prompt Library

An open-source collection of AI prompts built for truck rental customer service teams. Each prompt is designed to help support representatives respond faster and more consistently across the most common contact types in truck rental operations: reservations, scheduling, billing disputes, and complaint resolution.

This library is grounded in real-world contact centre workflows from high-volume truck and equipment rental operations, where representatives handle reservation changes, payment processing, dispute resolution, and multi-channel support (phone, email, live chat) under SLA and call quality targets.

## Why This Exists

Support teams in truck rental and logistics often rely on scripts and templates that are either too rigid or too generic to handle real customer situations. This library aims to close that gap by providing AI prompts that:

- Reflect the actual language and structure of real customer interactions
- Can be adapted directly into chatbots, internal copilots, or agent-assist tools
- Save time on repetitive but high-stakes interactions (billing disputes, refund requests, urgent dispatch)
- Stay consistent across agents and shifts, reducing variation in how issues get resolved

## Categories

### 1. Reservations
Prompts for creating, modifying, and confirming truck, trailer, and equipment reservations, including handling availability conflicts and recommending alternate pickup locations or equipment types.

**Example prompt:**
```
You are a customer service representative for a truck rental company.
A customer wants to extend their reservation by 2 days but the truck is
booked for another customer starting tomorrow. Politely explain the
conflict, offer two alternative solutions (different vehicle size or
nearby location), and ask which option works best for them.
```

### 2. Scheduling
Prompts for coordinating pickup and drop-off times, dispatch logistics, and equipment availability across locations.

**Example prompt:**
```
A customer needs to move their pickup time from 9 AM to 2 PM today.
Confirm whether the new time is feasible based on current equipment
availability, update the customer on any change in fees, and confirm
the new pickup details in writing.
```

### 3. Billing Disputes
Prompts for handling disputes related to late return fees, fuel charges, cleaning fees, and unexpected charges, including how to review account history and explain charges in plain language.

**Example prompt:**
```
A customer is disputing a $75 cleaning fee on their invoice, stating
the truck was returned clean. Ask for the details needed to review the
charge (reservation number, return date), explain how cleaning fees
are assessed, and outline the next steps for resolution if the fee
was applied in error.
```

### 4. Complaint Resolution
Prompts for de-escalating frustrated customers, documenting complaints accurately, and routing issues to the correct internal team (e.g., roadside assistance, corporate, sales).

**Example prompt:**
```
A customer is upset because their truck broke down during a move and
no one answered the roadside assistance line for 20 minutes. Acknowledge
their frustration without being defensive, confirm what immediate help
you can offer right now, and explain how the incident will be escalated
and followed up on.
```

## How to Use These Prompts

1. Copy a prompt from the relevant category above.
2. Adjust the bracketed or example details to match the real customer scenario.
3. Use the prompt with your AI tool of choice (Claude, ChatGPT, or an internal agent-assist tool).
4. Review and personalize the AI-generated response before sending it to a customer. These prompts are designed to support representatives, not replace human judgment on sensitive or high-stakes interactions.
